JacRentals is a heavy-equipment rental company based in Sulphur, Louisiana. We rent contractor-grade machines by the day, week, and month to builders, tradespeople, farms, and homeowners across Sulphur, Lake Charles, and the surrounding parishes.

What we rent

Our fleet covers the machines a job site actually needs:

  • Excavators & mini-excavators
  • Skid steers & track loaders
  • Dozers
  • Boom lifts & scissor lifts
  • Telehandlers & towable lifts
  • Rollers & plate compactors
  • Trenchers & augers
  • Generators, compressors & pumps
  • Utility trailers & tractors

How we work

Call, email, or stop by and we'll quote the right machine for your job, reserve it for your dates, and arrange delivery and pickup to your site. We handle the quote, reservation, delivery ETA, invoice, and return reminders so your crew can stay on the work.

Text-message (SMS) program

JacRentals sends service (transactional) text messages to rental customers who have given us their mobile number and opted in — so you never miss a quote, a return date, or a delivery. Messages relate only to your own rentals and account, and may include:

  • Rental quotes and totals
  • Reservation and return-date reminders
  • Delivery and pickup (ETA) alerts
  • Invoice and balance notices
  • Replies to questions you send us

How to opt in: visit our text-alerts sign-up page, enter your name and mobile number, and check the box agreeing to receive recurring service texts. Message frequency varies with your rental activity, and message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for help. Consent to receive texts is not a condition of any rental or purchase. We do not sell or share your mobile number or messaging consent with third parties for their marketing.
